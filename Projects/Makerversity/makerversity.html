About Makerversity

Makerversity is a pioneering community of emerging maker businesses. At our Somerset House HQ in central London and now, central Amsterdam, we are kickstarting the third industrial revolution. We provide access to a range of studio, event and fabrication spaces and prototyping tools for our diverse member businesses.
Our members also constitute our faculty, each contributing time and skills to inspire the next generation of creative and practical minds.
