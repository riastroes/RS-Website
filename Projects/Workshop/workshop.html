Workshops with Paintingmachines

In the weekend from 19 -21 august 2016 I did more than 50 workshops in the glasshouse at the Makerfestival in Amsterdam.

The participants were from 4 years old to about 72 years.
Everyone made a digital painting and I explained how the digital painting was made with creative coding (P5.Les.1: move your mouse in the field underneath the code.).
They learn how to paint circles at mouse point. The little robotmachines of the paintingmachines 16 app did a great job.
Young and old were amused by the funny machines.
